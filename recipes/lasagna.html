Lasagna

Home

Description

Can you imagine the cheese melting in your mouth? The recently squeezed tomatoes touching your nostrils? The various meats stemming with heat from a homemade lasagna?

Well, imagine it no more! Because you can make it in your home!

Ingredients

  • 6 Tomatoes
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Rosemary
  • 2 pounds of ground beef

Steps

  1. Prepare your oven
  2. Dice your ingredients
  3. Make the lasagna
  4. Put it in the oven
  5. Voila!
